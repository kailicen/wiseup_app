About

WisingUp is a open and safe place for you to harness your independent thinking skill. How does it work?

  • Ask questions: You actively ask questions that you are genuinely curious about.
  • Answer them: You spend a chunk of time to contemplate those questions from ground zero with your direct experience. Try not to speculate or use answers from other sources.
  • Do it consistently: Just like going to the gym, training your independent thinking 'muscle'.
  • You can either keep your questions and answers public or private in your profile setting. You are free to answer others' questions, too.

If you have any suggestions regarding to WiseUp, please feel free to email me (kailicen226@gmail.com).
